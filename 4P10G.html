Corelink Epon OLT 4 Port Dual Power 10G

The CoreLink "4 Port Dual Power 10G" specification likely means that this OLT has four ports for connecting to optical network units (ONUs) or optical network terminals (ONTs), and it supports 10 Gigabit Ethernet speeds on each port. Additionally, the "Dual Power" aspect suggests redundancy or backup power capability, providing reliability in case one power source fails.

Port Configuration:

The OLT has four ports supporting 10 Gigabit Ethernet (10G) speeds. These ports are used to connect to EPON ONUs or ONTs.

Gigabit Ethernet Support:

Each port supports Gigabit Ethernet (10Gbps) speeds. This enables high-speed data transmission between the OLT and the connected ONUs or ONTs, facilitating fast internet access for end-users.

Control Port:

Includes 1 x 10/100/1000M RJ45 port, 1 x Console (RJ45), and 1 x USB port for management and debugging purposes.
