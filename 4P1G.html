Corelink Epon OLT 4 Port Dual Power 1G

The "4 Port Dual Power 1G" specification likely indicates that this particular OLT model has four ports for connecting to ONUs or ONTs and supports Gigabit Ethernet (1Gbps) speeds. The "Dual Power" aspect suggests redundancy or failover capability, meaning the OLT can be powered by two separate power sources for increased reliability.

Port Configuration:

The OLT has four ports, which can be used to connect to EPON ONUs or ONTs. These ports serve as the interface between the OLT and the optical distribution network (ODN) that delivers connectivity to end-users.

Gigabit Ethernet Support:

Each port supports Gigabit Ethernet (1Gbps) speeds. This enables high-speed data transmission between the OLT and the connected ONUs or ONTs, facilitating fast internet access for end-users.

Dual Power Redundancy:

The OLT is equipped with dual power supply inputs, providing redundancy and ensuring continuous operation in case one power source fails. This feature enhances the reliability of the network infrastructure, minimizing downtime and ensuring uninterrupted service for end-users.
